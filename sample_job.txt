About the job
About the Role

We are looking for a motivated and curious Entry-Level Software Engineer to join our growing engineering team. In this role, you will work alongside experienced engineers to design, develop, and maintain scalable software solutions. This is a great opportunity to gain hands-on experience, learn modern technologies, and grow your career in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities

Assist in designing, developing, and testing software applications

Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code

Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product managers and designers

Debug, troubleshoot, and improve existing systems

Participate in code reviews and team discussions

Learn and adapt to new technologies and frameworks

Contribute to documentation and technical processes

Requirements

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience)

Strong understanding of programming fundamentals (e.g., Python, Java, C++, JavaScript)

Familiarity with data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented programming

Basic knowledge of web development, APIs, or databases is a plus

Good problem-solving and analytical skills

Strong communication and teamwork skills

Eagerness to learn and grow in a fast-paced environment

Preferred Qualifications (Nice to Have)

Internship or project experience in software development

Experience with frameworks like React, Node.js, or similar

Familiarity with version control systems like Git

Exposure to cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)

What We Offer

Competitive salary and benefits

Learning and mentorship opportunities

Flexible work environment (remote/hybrid options)

Opportunity to work on impactful real-world projects

Career growth and development support
